# Asset licensing

`LICENSE` (MIT) covers the **source code only** — the GDScript, the scenes,
and the Blender/tooling scripts under `tools/`.

The bundled **assets** — 3D models, textures, sound effects and music — are
**not** all MIT. Each is covered by its own license, summarised below. Where an
asset is a third-party work, its full attribution and source link live in the
per-folder `CREDITS.md` files; this page is the map, those files are the detail.

## Music and sound effects — generated with Suno Pro

Everything under `soundAndMusic/` was generated with a **Suno Pro**
subscription: the seven level tracks in `soundAndMusic/music/` **and** the
sound effects in `soundAndMusic/sounds/` (the death sound, the falling and
landing sounds, the ambient pad, the win jingles, the sand crunch). Under
Suno’s Terms of Service a paid subscriber is assigned Suno’s right, title and
interest in the output, which permits commercial use, distribution, and use in
this game. Two things follow from that:

- Suno makes **no warranty that any copyright vests** in the output. These files
  are therefore **not** offered under a free/libre or Creative Commons license,
  and are **not** covered by the MIT license or the CC-BY grant above. They are
  redistributed here under Suno’s terms only.
- For a **fully free/libre release** — one where every bundled asset carries a
  free license — **all of `soundAndMusic/`** (music and these SFX) would need
  replacing with CC0/CC-BY audio. The free-licensed effects in `sounds/` are
  unaffected.
- Suno’s terms: https://suno.com/terms-of-service
