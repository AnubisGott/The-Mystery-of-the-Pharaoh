The Mystery of the Pharaoh

Game Objective

The player walks along a path. He can only walk forward and sideways. He cannot leave the path. He can use the mouse to change his line of sight. After walking forward for a while, he reaches the goal and the game ends.

``` The complete process:

Start the game
→ Main menu
→ Enter the arena
→ Collect three energy cells
→ Exit opens
→ Reach the exit
→ Win
→ Restart or quit
