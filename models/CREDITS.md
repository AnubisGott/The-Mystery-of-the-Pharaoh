# Model credits

- `anubis.glb` — generated by `tools/blender/make_anubis.py`: the pedestal
  is lofted from and textured with the four-view artwork
  `textures/anubis/Anubis-low-res-all.jpg`; the jackal on top is decimated
  from ["Statuette of a Jackal"](https://sketchfab.com/3d-models/statuette-of-a-jackal-9aba58c860c7453c8bcea37dd614c288)
  by the **Art Institute of Chicago** (Sketchfab), licensed
  [CC Attribution 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).
  Source scan: `requirements/jackal-scan.glb`.
- `cat.glb` — generated by `tools/blender/make_cat.py`: decimated from
  ["Egyptian Cat Statue"](https://sketchfab.com/3d-models/egyptian-cat-statue-02b0456362f9442da46d39fb34b3ee5b)
  by **Ankledot** (Sketchfab), licensed
  [CC Attribution 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).
  Source model: `requirements/cat-scan.glb`.
- `crocodile.glb` — fully procedural, generated by
  `tools/blender/make_crocodile.py` (no external assets).
- `sarcophagus.glb` — generated by `tools/blender/make_sarcophagus.py`: a
  low-poly mummiform loft traced from and textured with the two-view
  artwork `textures/frontAndSideView-TAAS.jpg` (front + side).
- `scarab.glb` — decimated and rescaled by `tools/blender/make_scarab.py`
  from ["Egyptian scarab beetle"](https://sketchfab.com/3d-models/egyptian-scarab-beetle-b4cd7baabeea42e192c54eb02ca72c1a)
  by **Kudo** (Sketchfab), licensed [CC Attribution 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).
  Source scan: `requirements/scarab-scan.glb`.
- `sphinx.glb` — procedural body generated by `tools/blender/make_sphinx.py`.
  The head is cropped from ["The Great Sphinx of Giza - Egypt"](https://sketchfab.com/3d-models/the-great-sphinx-of-giza-egypt-f169dbe7974648babe327179091e0ee3)
  by **Chenzoss** (Sketchfab), licensed [CC Attribution 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).
  Source scan: `requirements/sphinx-scan.glb`.
- `adventurer.glb`, `pyramid.glb` — generated by scripts in `tools/blender/`.
  The adventurer's skeleton and animations come from the
  [KayKit Character Pack: Adventurers](https://github.com/KayKit-Game-Assets/KayKit-Character-Pack-Adventures-1.0)
  by **Kay Lousberg** (CC0); the source rig is committed as
  `tools/blender/assets/kaykit_rig.glb`, the mesh is replaced by
  `make_adventurer.py` and the crouch clips are authored there.
- `adventurer_realistic.glb` — generated by `tools/blender/make_adventurer_realistic.py`
  (Level 1 character). Body generated with [MPFB2](https://static.makehumancommunity.org/mpfb.html)
  (MakeHuman community, MIT-licensed tool; generated characters are CC0).
  Animations from the [Universal Animation Library](https://quaternius.com/packs/universalanimationlibrary.html)
  by **Quaternius** (CC0), committed as `tools/blender/assets/ual_animation_library.glb`.
  Clothes from the MakeHuman community asset packs (CC0):
  polo shirt by **namuhekam**, cargo pants by **cortu**, boots by
  **culturalibre** — committed under `tools/blender/assets/mhclo/`.
  The fedora is generated procedurally.
- `adventurer_modular.glb` — generated by `tools/blender/make_adventurer_modular.py`.
  Body: the "Adventurer" from the
  [Ultimate Modular Men Pack](https://poly.pizza/bundle/Ultimate-Modular-Men-Pack-ZiH8muWqwQ)
  by **Quaternius** (CC0), committed as `tools/blender/assets/umm_adventurer.glb`.
  Animations retargeted from the Quaternius Universal Animation Library (CC0).
  (Alternative Level-2 character, kept switchable.)
- `adventurer_tactical.glb` — generated by `tools/blender/make_adventurer_tactical.py`
  (current Level 2 character). MPFB2-generated body (MakeHuman community, CC0 output),
  Quaternius Universal Animation Library animations (CC0). Outfit — MakeHuman community
  asset packs, committed under `tools/blender/assets/mhclo/`:
  - CC0: crude t-shirt by **Elvaerwyn** (shirts01), cargo pants by **cortu** (pants01),
    hero boots by **culturalibre** (shoes01), and the natural body skin "Aksel" by
    **Mindfront** (skins02), committed under `tools/blender/assets/skin/`.
  - CC-BY: tactical vest by **Mindfront** and sling purse by **Elvaerwyn** (equipment03),
    trilby hat by **Elvaerwyn** (hats03) — see https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/
